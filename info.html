IP Address: What is it and why does it matter?

A complete guide to Network Identification and Online Privacy.

What is an IP Address?

An IP (Internet Protocol) address is a unique numerical label assigned to each device connected to a computer network. It acts like your home address on the internet.

Security Tip: Your public IP address reveals information about your Internet Service Provider (ISP) and your approximate geographic location.

Why monitor your IP?

Knowing your IP address is essential for several technical and security tasks like VPN verification and network configuration.
